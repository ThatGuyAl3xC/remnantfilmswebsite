Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about tinting, wrapping, and more.

How long does window tint take to install?
Most standard vehicles take about 2–3 hours to tint. Time may vary depending on the number of windows and the complexity of the job.
Is window tint legal in California?
Yes, but California law restricts tint darkness on the front side windows. We always comply with state regulations and offer legal options.
How long will my vinyl wrap last?
A high-quality vinyl wrap can last 5–7 years with proper care. Factors like sun exposure, washing habits, and parking conditions can affect lifespan.
Can I take my car through a car wash after tinting or wrapping?
After tinting, wait 3–5 days before washing. For wraps, avoid high-pressure washes and harsh chemicals to prolong the material’s life.
Do you offer warranties for your services?
Yes. We offer manufacturer warranties on tint and vinyl, and our work is backed by a satisfaction guarantee. Specific terms depend on the product.
Can a vinyl wrap damage my paint?
No, as long as your paint is in good condition. In fact, wraps can protect your original paint from scratches and sun damage.
How do I maintain my tint or wrap?
Use gentle, ammonia-free cleaners and soft cloths. Avoid scraping or pressure washing. We'll give you care tips when your job is done.

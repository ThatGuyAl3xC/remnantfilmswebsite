Window Tinting

Professional Protection & Style

Why Tint Your Windows?

At Remnant Films & Customs, we offer top-quality window tinting that provides both aesthetics and protection. Tinting your vehicle enhances privacy, reduces interior heat, blocks harmful UV rays, and protects your upholstery from fading.

Benefits of Our Window Tinting:

  • ✔ Blocks up to 99% of harmful UV rays
  • ✔ Reduces heat for a cooler cabin
  • ✔ Increases privacy and security
  • ✔ Enhances your vehicle’s appearance
  • ✔ Lifetime warranty on select films

We offer a variety of tint shades and film types—from standard dyed film to advanced ceramic tint—to match your style and budget. All installations are done with precision and care to ensure a flawless, bubble-free finish.

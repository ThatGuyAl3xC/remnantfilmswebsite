Window Tinting

⚜️ Professional Protection & Style Tailored for You ⚜️

Precision Window Tinting for Every Vehicle

At Remnant Films & Customs, our window tinting service is more than just an upgrade—it's a shield. We use high-quality, durable film options that not only improve the look of your ride but also enhance its performance, comfort, and safety.

Tailored Tinting Consultations & Fast Installations

Whether you're seeking privacy, UV protection, or just a sleek new look, we provide personalized consultations to help you choose the best film for your needs. Our skilled technicians work efficiently to get your tint installed quickly without sacrificing quality.

Protect & Preserve Your Investment

Quality tinting helps preserve your car’s interior by blocking harmful sunlight that causes fading and cracking. Our films also protect passengers from excessive heat and UV exposure, making every drive more enjoyable and safer.

Privacy, Safety, and Shatter Resistance

Our premium tints help obscure your belongings from outside view and hold shattered glass together in case of an accident, offering added protection for you and your passengers. It's not just about looking great—it's about peace of mind.

Let's Get You Scheduled

Ready to upgrade your vehicle? Contact us for a free consultation and flexible scheduling. Our team will walk you through film options, shade levels, and care instructions. We're here to ensure you get the most value and satisfaction from your tint.

  • ✔ Legal compliance with California tint laws
  • ✔ Multiple shades and film options (dyed, carbon, ceramic)
